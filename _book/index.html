Preface

This work contains some Bayesian model examples in Stan.

At the moment, the majority of these are models are derived from the Simon Jackman’s “BUGS Examples: 19 worked examples in WinBUG”, lasted updated on December 4, 2009, written by Simon Jackman and previously available on his website.
