16 Engines: right-censored failure times

Forty motors were tested at various operating temperatures, and failure times were noted; 23 of the motors had not failed at the end of the observational period (i.e., the failure times for these observations are right-censored) (Tanner 1996). Substantive interest is in the regression of failure times on operating temperature; the statistical issue is how to obtain estimates of the regression parameters given the right-censoring.
